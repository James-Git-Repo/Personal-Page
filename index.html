About me

Hi, I’m Jacopo. Swiss–Italian analyst and builder based between Swiss and Northern Italy. I help Italian SMEs and startups land in Switzerland and scale — especially in fintech, med‑tech and AI‑powered products.

I turn messy signals into crisp, institutional‑grade briefs and ship small tools that remove friction: from a Google Drive workflow that auto‑sorts documents to a 1‑million‑slot AI billboard project. I care about clarity, speed, and usefulness.

Highlights: coordinated the ICE collective at Swiss Biotech Day 2025; drafted MDR/IVDR compliance notes for med‑tech; provided guidance to SMEs and startups on Swiss market entry; and built European Market Movers to make market updates fast to read and easy to share.
